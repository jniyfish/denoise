Salt And Pepper Noise

Medium Filter

Salt and pepper noise, also known as impulse noise, is a kind of noise that is often seen in many researches. This type of noise can be caused by sudden and severe interference from the image signal, such as the failure of pixel elements in camera sensors, faulty memory space in storage or analog-to-digital converter error and many more. The noisy image of salt and pepper will have maximum (255) or minimum (0) pixel values which appear a random white or black point on an image.

Speckle Noise

Bilateral Filter

Speckle noise is a type of noise commonly found in medical tomography images and synthetic aperture radar (SAR), and it generally tends to reduce the image contrast. Early scientists often used multiple observations to removal speckle and improve the interpretation of radar images. Speckle noise is a kind of multiplicative noise and appearance has a granular pattern.

Gaussian noise is a type of noise whose probability density function is equal to the noise of a Gaussian distribution, also known as normal distribution which is a very important probability distribution in the fields of mathematics, physics and engineering.
